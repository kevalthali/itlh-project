SPECIAL OCCASIONS

EVERY STEP OF THE WAY

Whoever you are, wherever you go, you can be sure that there is a pair of Undandys for each and every occasion.

Therefore, permit us to suggest some standout styles so you can be sure

you are putting your best foot forward.

 
What’s the occasion?

That important business meeting is looming and you want to make a great first impression.

Though we can’t guarantee our shoes will give you the promotion you deserve, you’ll be sure to start things off on the right foot with the ‘15 by Powell. They say dress for the job you want, not the job you have and with this half brogue Oxford, you’ll certainly be saying ‘Show me the money’.

Date night. After all, one, should always keep them on their toes.

Whether you’re taking your partner out to your favourite restaurant to pop the question, a hot date or simply want to keep the magic alive, you’re bound to impress in the ‘48 by Michael Corelone. Beautifully handcrafted in black calfskin leather with tan soles, this classically stylish pair of Derby shoes will definitely keep them wanting more.
